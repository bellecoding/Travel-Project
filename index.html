Why São Paulo?

São Paulo, the beating heart of Brazil

São Paulo isn’t just Brazil’s largest city — it’s a vibrant cultural powerhouse, home to more than 12 million people and a melting pot of ideas, flavors, and traditions. From Japanese influences in Liberdade to Italian roots in Bixiga, the city celebrates diversity in every corner.

Take a stroll down Avenida Paulista, the city’s most iconic avenue, where skyscrapers meet museums like MASP and lush parks offer a break from the buzz. Explore the famous Municipal Market for pastel and mortadella sandwiches, or catch a football match at Morumbi Stadium. Whether you're into art, food, nightlife, or urban adventures, São Paulo has something for everyone — always buzzing, always bold.
